About Us

Za Pool Resort is located in Sap-ang, Silago Southern Leyte. It is a convenient two hours drive from Tacloban City. The resort features only oceanfront accommodations sprawled out and nestled amidst a variety of trees, designed by nature so that one enjoys the utmost privacy. With the serene and pristine waters and some rocks and precious shells, one can easily view the sunrise and sunset. It is a tranquil and natural experience reminiscent of beaches in the old days. So make your special moments even more memorable in a private beach. Whether it's a celebration of an event, seminars, team building, we are sure to make this event a memorable one. If you are appreciative of such a place, then come, spend the day with us, pack your beach costume and experience the scenic and alluring site of Za Pool de Baie Beach Resort.
